«Limited Reserve» is a new series of special-blend pipe tobaccos available only in limited quantities. In future, «Limited Reserve» pipe tobaccos will be presented as the highlight of the year for ambitious pipe smokers. The first blend in the series is called «Sweet Mixture No.1» and will go on sale in an attractive round tin containing 100 g of tobacco. Only 3’000 tins are to go on sale worldwide. The Davidoff «Sweet Mixture No.1» is made of choice, well aged Virginia tobaccos combined with two different fermented Black Cavendish tobaccos. To round off the blend, it is refined with a touch of roasted Burley, making for a harmonious, well-rounded taste. This mild, slightly sweet, loose-cut blend will delight on account of its understated vanilla aroma and hintsof dried fruit, which together make for a pleasant-smelling smoke.
