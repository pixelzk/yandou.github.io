详细介绍
A selection of fine, ripe Virginia tobaccos is enhanced with the perfect measure of Maduro cigar leaf to provide an alluring spice, the suggestion of the richness of a great cigar, and a creamy smoke. A little Cyprian Latakia adds roundness and smoothes the edges, while fine Oriental tobaccos fill in the gaps. Complex and full flavored, yet very gentle on the tongue, Robusto is perfect with afternoon coffee, as a delicious finish to a wonderful meal, or as a stout companion throughout the day.
网友点评
初吸一口，有种窜鼻的感觉。但几口后回味醇厚，香浓，有种放马牧场的辽阔感！一斗到底层次变化明显，由惊艳到醇香再到最后的飘飘然然。回味无穷∞
感觉厚重醇香带点甜香 很醉人 值得一试
劲道中至强，味甘（不是甜）生津（对，就是这么好！），抽的过程中鼻内有淡淡的雪茄香，抽后鼻腔内“臭臭”的，享受。室韵对于他人来讲确实是臭烘烘的，但对我来讲是宜人的“臭”，忍不住作乌龟吐纳状、狗狗嗅味状而恋恋不舍。
