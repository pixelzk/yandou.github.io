详细介绍
A blend of the best English tradition. Blended in aged copper drums. Made from the best tobaccos in the world: Red Kentucky, Virginia, Turkish, Carolina Black and Syrian Latakia. Mild to medium taste with an unobtrusive smoky note. A blend for pipe smokers who know to appreciate an unsweetened English tobacco.
