详细介绍
Once you light up with HH Mature Virginia the sweet Virginia tobaccos will greet you and slowly the dark fired Virginia will enter the taste. Over time experience how the complex naturally sweet and slightly smoky taste grows in power making HH Mature Virginia a satisfying blend to smoke - and with no tongue bite.
