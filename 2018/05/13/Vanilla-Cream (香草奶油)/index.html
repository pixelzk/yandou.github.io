详细介绍
Mac Baren Vanilla Cream is a colorfull blend mixed with cut plugs. Manufactured from specially selected and aged Virginia and mild black Cavendish tobaccos. Then blended with an exceptional Vanilla Flavor. This selection creates an outstanding blend with superior flavor, unique aroma and distinctive taste experience.
