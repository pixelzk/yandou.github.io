详细介绍
Lemon yellow, red and dark Virginias are combined with broad cut black Cavendish.

A little hint of Burley, rounded with granulates of Virginia tobacco gives this mixture an extravagant aroma.

All together these splendid exotic aromas of mellow mango in combination with Caribbean rum permeate the senses and result in a marvellous smoking experience.

檸檬黃色，紅色和深弗吉尼亞結合具有廣闊的切割黑卡文迪什。

有點淡淡的白肋煙與弗吉尼亞煙草顆粒圓潤給出了這樣的混合物奢華的香氣。

全部在一起，這些燦爛的異國情調的結合加勒比朗姆酒醇厚的芒果香味滲透在一個奇妙的吸煙體驗的感官和結果。 |
