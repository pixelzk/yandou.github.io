详细介绍
As the name implies, this is a mild, smooth, rich pipe tobacco that improves throughout the smoke, owing to the slow release of the flavors inherent in the top quality leaf used in the blend. Burley, Cavendish and Black Cavendish with flavors hinting at pecans and honey.
