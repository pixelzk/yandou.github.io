详细介绍
A very harmonious Ready rubbed tobacco dominated by selected Burley tobaccos and with aminor part of Virginia and the original Mac Baren Cavendish. In this blend the tobaccos are adjusted to bring out the tender note of chocolate - which is found in all excellent Burley tobacco.
网友点评
烟劲道比较强，有瘾的朋友可能会喜欢，但我没有烟瘾，所以觉得太冲了。室韵一般，不香到也不是很臭，抽在嘴里有点像板栗味，抽过以后嘴里还能有一股淡淡的烟草味。这个调配还是不如不试，特别不适合新手。
