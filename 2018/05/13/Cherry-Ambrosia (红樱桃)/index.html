详细介绍
To achieve the delightful taste in this blend, Mac Baren has selected some very special Virginia tobaccos and added Ready Rubbed Burley tobacco. The taste is adjusted with their modern Cavendish made from pure Virginia tobaccos. As a final touch, genuine Jamaica rum and a special cherry flavor have been added. This blend gives you a cool and pleasant smoke with a taste of delightful cherries.
